Gultik Blok M

Kuliner

Access: (8.9/10)| Place: (8.5/10)| Delicious: (9.1/10)|

Hey guys, back again at 6Seasons Productions. This time we will discuss food again, namely Gultik or Curry Bends in the Blok M area. So this food is in the Blok M area, to be precise behind the Blok M Plaza. To go there, you can use private vehicles or public transportation such as the MRT & Transjakarta. Parking is a little difficult because it's crowded. Oh yes, the opening hours start from 17.00 WIB until around 01.00 or 02.00 WIB. Yes, that's more or less the information we got, so let's go straight to rating.


So first, we'll talk about access as usual. The access itself is fairly easy because it's close to the MRT station & Transjakarta bus stop. Then for parking, you can park at Blok M Square and Blok M Plaza, then from there you can walk a little to the Bend Curry. So our score for this access is (8.9/10). Next for the place, for the place you can say it's enough to eat. The place to eat is standard and it's on the side of the road, there are seats and small tables as usual (Gultik in Blok M are many and lined up so you don't have to worry about not getting enough). In the Gultik area there are also many other small snacks. So our score for this place to eat is (8.5/10). The last one is delicacy, for this delicacy you can say it's delicious even though the portion and price are a little bit disproportionate. 1 portion (containing rice, crackers, gravy & meat) costs around 10 thousand. Each place also sells various types of satay such as intestines, lungs, skin, eggs and many more (the price is around 5 thousand). So our score for this food is (9.1/10), a little expensive but worth it to buy.
