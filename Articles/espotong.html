Es Potong mas Deddy

Kuliner

Access: (9.4/10)| Place: (9.4/10)| Delicious: (9.5/10)|

Hey guys, back again at 6Seasons Productions. In this article, we will review culinary again but not heavy food (eg rice, meat and others). So what we are going to review is an old snack, namely Es Potong. The location is again located in Pasar Baru, the details are close to a beautiful model gold shop. Usually there from noon around 12.00 WIB until around 16.00/17.00 WIB. There are 5 flavors available.


Again and again, as usual it's time for a review session. First, in terms of access to places, it's pretty easy to get to places like Pasar Baru & Bakmi Gang Kelinci. Score from us (9.4/10). Then for the place....... we can't judge because it's in the form of a cart and it's on the side of the road, so you can eat while walking around. But the cart itself is clean, so you don't need to be afraid of getting dirty, our score is for the place (9.4/10). The last one is whether it's delicious or not. As for delicacy, we can say that it is perfect from us, the reason is because the taste of the fruit itself is very sweet and even tastes using pure sugar, in terms of taste it is also not inferior, which has chocolate, jackfruit, avocado, black sticky rice and green beans. The portion is also decent, where the price is around 5 thousand you can get a piece with medium size. Our score for taste (9.5/10).
