Pasar Baru

Wisata

Access: (7.8/10) | Place: (9/10) | Facility: (7.5/10) |

Hey guys, back again at 6Seasons Productions. On this occasion, we will share our experience of traveling to Pasar Baru, which is located in the Sawah Besar district, Central Jakarta. A little history about Pasar Baru, Pasar Baru is certainly inseparable from the Dutch colonial glory in the mid-20th century. Formerly called Passer Baroe, the colonial government built the oldest shopping center area in Jakarta since 1820.That was a brief history of Pasar Baru, back to the info we provided. So Pasar Baru itself is open from 07.00 WIB - 17.00 WIB, but if you want to come I recommend more at 09.00 because on average all the shops there just open around that time. In Pasar Baru itself, there are many places that you can visit, such as the famous culinary delights, namely Bakmi Gang Kelinci, then there is a thrifting place, there is a place for camera service ranging from DSLR/Mirrorless (even lenses can do that too), and many more. There is also a parking space itself, but it's a little difficult if you want to park there because it's crowded, but don't worry, because you can park your vehicle at Metro Atom Plaza (this building is on the opposite side of Pasar Baru).


Noted:

We also have info for those of you who like street photography, this place can be made into one of these objects. But to do that, you need permission from security (the method is quite easy, you only need to go to the security post and fill in the guest book, if there is no security, you can ask the security around there).


As usual, then we will review the whole of Pasar Baru. So the first is the parking lot, for the parking lot itself there are 2 places but they also have their own advantages and disadvantages. The first is in Pasar Barunya & the second is in Metro Atom Plaza. In Pasar Baru, the advantage is that it is practical and the fares are also flat. The drawback is that the parking lot is very close to other motorbikes. Whereas in Metro Atom Plaza it is wider and more spacious, only the cost is calculated per hour and the drawback is that you have to cross first to get to Pasar Baru (in our opinion it is not practical for the elderly). So our rating is for parking (7.8/10). The second is the whole place, for the place itself it's pretty cool and you can say it's not expensive. Here there is also a place for trifthing whose prices vary & are cheap (there are even those that cost 20 thousand). Culinary in here is also a lot and delicious. For those of you who want to go to the mini market, it's also here. So from us the score is (9/10). Lastly, about our own facilities, maybe you could say it's not bad. The toilets are a bit complicated because you have to enter the Pasar Baru Mall area or you can go to the existing minimarket. The rest is for security (security) and for the information that is there too, but not much. From us get a score (7.5/10).
