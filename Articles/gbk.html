Hutan Kota GBK

Wisata

Access: 8/10 | Place: 8.8/10 | Facility: 8.5/10 |

Hey guys, back again at 6Seasons Productions. This time we will review the urban forest in Jakarta, namely the GBK urban forest. The GBK urban forest is of course located at gate 7 of GBK Senayan (just for your info, there are 2 urban forests, namely the urban forest by Plataran and the urban forest by GBK). The opening hours are divided into 2 sessions, namely session 1 which is open from 06.00 WIB - 10.00 WIB & session 2 which is open from 15.00 WIB - 18.00 WIB, apart from those hours the urban forest is closed. Around the urban forest area there are also several areas that can be visited such as a jogging track that goes around the GBK stadium, then there are the Aquatic and Istora stadiums which are usually used for events. Other places have very complete fields such as basketball courts, archery, hockey, softball and many more. Then to access there yourself you can use private vehicles or public transportation (keep in mind that if you use a private vehicle you must have an electronic card such as flazz & e-money, and make sure your balance is sufficient). If you use public transportation, such as the busway, you can get off at the Gelora Bung Karno stop.


Next, we will provide an overall rating for this gbk urban forest. So the first is access there & a parking space, we give it (8/10). Actually, for the parking lots themselves, there are many and very wide for each parking space, but there are some obstacles that in my opinion are lacking, such as needing an electronic card/money to park, so it's a bit inconvenient if you don't have an electronic card. The second is the location of the urban forest. For the urban forest area itself, we give it a score of (8.8/10) also because the place is clean and organized, there are also lots of trash cans there, information boards there are also sufficient. The view here is also very good, namely the building. A little information also that this place can be used for picnics, dating is also recommended. (note: if you want to be quieter and more comfortable, you can come in the morning for session 1) The third or last one is the facilities, the facilities here can be said to be quite adequate. here there are toilets and a prayer room which are quite neat and clean, and as mentioned earlier there are also a lot of information boards and trash cans here. Outside the urban forest, there is also a canteen/small shop that sells a variety of food and drinks. For that we give a score (8.5/10)
