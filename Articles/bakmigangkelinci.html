Bakmie Gang Kelinci

Kuliner

Access: (8.9/10) | Place: (9/10) | Delicious: (6.8/10)|

Hey guys, back again at 6Seasons Productions. On this occasion we will review food from Bakmi Gang Kelinci which is located on Jl. Kelinci Raya, No.1-3, Ps. Baru, Sawah Besar District, Central Jakarta City. So Bakmi Gang Kelinci is near or you could say it's still in the Pasar Baru area. Opening hours start at 09.00 WIB, and close at 20.00 WIB. For your own parking, you can park in front of Bakmi Gang Kelinci or you can park around Pasar Baru (if you park around Pasar Baru, you have to walk a little), for the access is pretty easy. The menu menu here can also be considered complete, starting from noodles, fried rice, vermicelli, kwetiaw and many more.


And as usual, we'll move on to scoring. The first is access, the access itself can be said to be easy because it's easy to find a parking space, and if you want to use public transportation it's also easy. So our score is (8.9/10). Next we discuss the place, for the place it can be said to be large. Then from another perspective you could say this is comfortable because the room is not hot and not cramped, eating with relatives or friends here is also comfortable. So the score we give is (9/10). Finally, the most important thing is the deliciousness of the food itself. As for the noodles themselves, in our opinion, the taste is just normal like noodles in general, but what we quite like is the texture. The texture is soft and of course makes you full, the point is that the food is enough (not very good & not very bad either). For ordinary drinks nothing special. Our score for food and drink is (6.8/10).

(Note: We ordered Special Noodles, Loucupan & Fried Dumplings And for the drinks, sweet iced tea, iced tea & coffee)
